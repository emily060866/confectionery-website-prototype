What is Beau's Bonbons?

Beau is a 100% vegan confectionary store that started back in 2009 in Montreal, Canada. Today, our delicacies are now intermationally appreciated. The founder of Beau's Bonbons. Noel Beau, is and has been a vegan all his life and wants to promote his lifestyle to more and more people.
